Japan

Japan is an island nation in East Asia known for its unique blend of ancient tradition and cutting-edge modernity. From the serene temples of Kyoto and the iconic slopes of Mount Fuji to the neon-lit streets of Tokyo, the country offers a striking contrast of old and new. Renowned for its rich culture, delicious cuisine — including sushi, ramen, and wagyu beef — and the concept of omotenashi (wholehearted hospitality), Japan is a place where precision, craftsmanship, and harmony are deeply valued in everyday life.
