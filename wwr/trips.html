Featured Trips

Arkansas River: Family Float

A great first rafting experience for the whole family. This half-day trip features gentle Class II and III rapids through a beautiful canyon. Kids as young as 6 are welcome, and our guides make sure everyone stays safe and has a blast.

⏱ Half Day 👶 Ages 6+ 💲 $79/person

Colorado River: Royal Gorge

Our most popular trip! Raft through the dramatic Royal Gorge with towering canyon walls on both sides. The river throws Class IV and V rapids at you all day long. No experience needed — just a sense of adventure and a willingness to get very wet.

⏱ Full Day 👶 Ages 14+ 💲 $149/person

Gauley River: Beast of the East

Only for experienced rafters — the Gauley is one of the hardest commercial whitewater rivers in the world. The fall dam releases create nonstop Class V action through legendary rapids. This one will push you to your absolute limit.


⏱ Full Day 👶 Ages 18+ 💲 $179/person
